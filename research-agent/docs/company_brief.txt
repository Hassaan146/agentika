INTERNAL BRIEF — AI COMPANY PROFILE
Prepared for the Week 4 research demo.

Company: Anthropic
Founded: 2021, headquartered in San Francisco, USA.

Overview:
Anthropic is an AI safety and research company. Its flagship product family is
the Claude series of large language models. The company is known for its work
on constitutional AI, a training approach that aligns model behavior with an
explicit set of written principles, and for interpretability research aimed at
understanding what happens inside large models.

Products:
- Claude models, served via API and consumer apps.
- Claude Code, an agentic coding tool that operates in the terminal and IDEs.

Notes:
The company was founded by former members of another prominent AI research
laboratory. Leadership details are intentionally omitted from this brief and
should be researched separately.
